Varisankya 3.9-beta.15 - sign-in reliability.

Fixes for signing in: if Google sign-in fails, the app now shows the real reason on screen instead of silently doing nothing, plus a more robust "Sign in with Google" fallback so you can get back in after being signed out.

If you were logged out and couldn't sign back in, please update and try again - and share the on-screen message if it still fails.
